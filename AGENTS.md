# AGENTS.md — thesis

Academic thesis: GIS-based analysis of commuter facilities and modern jeepney routes using contingency valuation and K-means clustering in Cagayan de Oro City (Barangay Gusa to Barangay Bugo).

## Thesis details

- **Authors:** Lopez, Usman, Capiña, Cundangan
- **Institution:** Xavier University – Ateneo de Cagayan, BS Civil Engineering
- **Date:** July 2026
- **Study area:** 6 barangays along the National Highway — Gusa, Cugman, Tablon, Agusan, Puerto, Bugo
- **Methods:** GIS spatial mapping, K-means clustering (3 clusters: high/moderate/low demand), Contingent Valuation Method (CVM), Willingness to Pay (WTP)
- **Sample:** 385 commuters
- **Key outputs:** priority waiting shed locations (Bugo Public Market and Puerto National Highway ranked highest); mean WTP = ₱4.72/trip
